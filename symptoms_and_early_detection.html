Symptoms & Early Detection


CDC guidance on exposure risk assessment

Anecdotal reports have indicated that the illness seems to creep up on some patients with what might start out as something comparable to atypical pneumonia and can quickly take a turn for the worse.


Hospitals in New York have been identifying potential cases requiring hospitalization through CT scans. 1
In China in some cases CT scans were used to identify presumptive positives with a low rate (3.9 %) of misdiagnosis. 2
For more information on how COVID-19 presents on CT scans see this anaylsis of 121 patients.

Some have suggested that abdominal pain is an often overlooked early symptom of COVID-19. 3 4 5 In this New York Times video, an NYC nurse reveals patients come in with stomach aches and are identified COVID-19.
